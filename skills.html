Skills & Business Ideas

Digital Skills Worth Learning

  • Basic Excel & Data Analysis
  • Graphic design (Canva, Figma)
  • AutoCAD / SolidWorks (for engineers)
  • Electrical installations & maintenance
  • Freelancing skills (Upwork, Fiverr)

How to Learn Effectively

  1. Define a 30-day plan
  2. Use free resources (YouTube, Coursera, freeCodeCamp)
  3. Practice small projects and build a portfolio
  4. Join field-specific groups for mentorship

Business Ideas for Graduates

Service businesses with low capital: mobile phone repair, small fabrication, tutoring/classes, freelance digital services.
